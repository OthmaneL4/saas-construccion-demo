# LinkedIn Portfolio Post

## English Version

I built a public demo of a SaaS-style business management platform for construction contractors and small building companies.

Live demo: https://lsototalbouw-demo.onrender.com
GitHub: https://github.com/OthmaneL4/saas-construccion-demo

The application centralizes daily business operations in one place:

- Customers and projects
- Quotations and invoices
- Payments and receivables
- Expenses and profitability
- Materials and tools
- Documents and calendar planning
- Work logs, users, roles, notifications, and audit history

The project was built with Java, Spring Boot, Spring MVC, Spring Security, Spring Data JPA, Hibernate, Thymeleaf, Bootstrap, Flyway, H2 for the public demo, and MySQL-ready production configuration.

The public environment is intentionally read-only and uses fictional data only. The goal is to demonstrate product thinking, clean enterprise architecture, security awareness, operational documentation, and production-oriented engineering practices.
